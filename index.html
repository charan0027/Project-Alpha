About Me

I am an urbanist driven by a simple question, 'Why can’t we use technology to solve the daily challenges of city living?' I view cities as vibrant economic hubs, yet they face complex hurdles in infrastructure, mobility, and security.

My mission is to leverage Artificial Intelligence and emerging technologies to solve urban problems. With a background in Urban Design and Planning, I apply systems thinking and policy-making to create sustainable, inclusive, and futuristic urban spaces. I am actively seeking opportunities to apply my expertise in AI-driven urban interventions to improve citizen life and rejuvenate our cities.
